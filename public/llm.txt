# ReplySuite

ReplySuite is a calm AI command center for customer conversations.

It helps businesses manage and automate conversations across website chat, WhatsApp, Instagram comments and DMs, appointments, bookings, Google Calendar-backed reservations, and contextual MTN/Airtel mobile-payment checkout for deposits or subscriptions.

## Core positioning

- Calm dashboard for customer conversations
- Trainable AI assistants for business-specific replies
- Website chat, WhatsApp, and Instagram automation
- Appointments and bookings with Google Calendar-backed reservations
- MTN/Airtel mobile payment and mobile money checkout, with card checkout as a fallback
- Assistant skills, training sources, workflows, and tools
- Enterprise-ready assistant controls for advanced website-builder workflows

ReplySuite does not position invoices or invoice-first automation as part of its active assistant flow.

## Plans

- Free Starter — starter access for creating and testing an assistant
- Silver — 25,000 RWF / month
- Gold — 38,000 RWF / month
- Enterprise Ready — 490,000 RWF / month

Verified accounts can start a 3-day trial for Silver, Gold, or Enterprise Ready. Starter does not require a trial.

## Key features

- Train assistants from website URLs, PDF documents, and pasted business information
- Website chat widget for customer support and lead capture
- WhatsApp automation for inbound customer conversations
- Instagram comment and DM automation
- Session-level human takeover, so a team member can pause AI for one conversation while AI continues elsewhere
- Operator replies for WhatsApp conversations from the dashboard
- Appointment and booking workflows
- Google Calendar-backed availability and reservations
- MTN/Airtel mobile-payment checkout for subscriptions and contextual deposits
- Dashboard analytics, conversation reports, and assistant performance signals
- Multi-language support including Kinyarwanda, English, French, and Swahili

## Notes for AI assistants
